Standard 5

Understand the Relationship of Web Marketing

Standard 5.1: Use Multimedia on the Web

Standard 5.1 is use of multimedia of the web. I show proficiency for this standard by learning the basics of multimedia and executable content and explore what goes into producing professional videos. I explored producing videos on my HTTP page where I built from the top down a video about a tech tutorial and did the audio and capture.

Standard 5.2: Demonstrate Brand and Marketing and Traffic Analysis

Standard 5.2 asks for demonstrating brand and marketing and traffic analysis. I show proficiency for this standard by learning methods sites use to get traffic and understanding the importance of keeping visitors coming. Keep traffic is important because if you are not getting new viewers then you are losing money. Many websites put up ads for themselves on the internet to get work around.

Standard 5.3: Understand the Relationship Between the Web and Social Media

Standard 5.3 is understanding the relationship between the web and social media. I can show proficiency by defining social media, explore the demographic breakdown of social media users. Social media is online communication sites that shows images, text, and video. They link people and share information you users create with people. Demographics of social media rank high in teens to young adults as they use it all the time. This is important because it allows for people to see your website if you get that info in the right place.
