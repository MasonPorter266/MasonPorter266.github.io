Standard 4

Understand Web Related Planning and Organizational Standards

Standard 4.1: Demonstrate Understanding of Website Architecture and Planning

Standard 4.1 asks for demonstrating an understanding of website architecture and planning. I am able to show proficiency for standard 4.1 by the beginning stages of website development which are creating a wireframe to map out the look of a site and flowcharts which show the navigation of the site and publishing my website which is used in my Semester Project website.

Standard 4.2: Demonstrate Understanding of Site Organization and Navigation Principles

Standard 4.2 focuses on demonstrating understanding of site organization and navigation principles. I am able to show proficiency for this standard through building a horizontal navigation bar, usable navigation, and use of background colors to enhance navigation which is demonstrated in my HTTP website.

Standard 4.3: Demonstrate understanding of Web Site Accessibility Standards

Standard 4.3 is looking for the ability to demonstrate understanding of website accessibility. I show proficiency by understanding what the accessibility standards are. The accessibility standards are a set of rules of thumb when creating a site which aim to provide extra help to those who may have a disability when viewing your page. Some rules of thumb are using alt tags and color contrast that is not similar.
