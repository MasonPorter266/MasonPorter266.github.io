How to Technology Tutorials is a website dedicated to helping users learn how to use the world of technology and give tips and tricks for maintaining technology.

We create and edit our videos in house and our goal is to provide top quality videos to all of our users. We take suggestions on what to do next from our users so if you have an idea for a tutorial it can be submitted through our contact form.

We upload videos every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

We post updates on videos and also take ideas from Facebook, Twitter, and Youtube so if you enjoy what you see like and share us.

One on one tech support is avaliable through our help form. You can also ask for help on Facebook and Twitter.

A big thanks to all of our supporters for helping to keep us going and provide quality uploads.

If you see any bugs or problems with the website please submit them in the help form.
